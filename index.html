Australia PR Pathways for Indians

01

Skilled Independent Visa (Subclass 189)

A points-based permanent residency visa for highly skilled professionals. No employer or state sponsorship required.

Direct PR Work anywhere in Australia
02

Skilled Nominated Visa (Subclass 190)

Permanent residency for skilled workers nominated by an Australian state or territory government.

State nomination Extra PR points
03

Skilled Work Regional Visa (Subclass 491)

A regional pathway that allows skilled migrants to live and work in designated regional areas with a route to PR.

Regional benefits PR pathway

Australia PR Process Guidance

Explore different pathways to Australian permanent residency.

Step 1: Check Eligibility

The Australia PR process starts with eligibility. We ensure your profile qualifies before applying.

Step 2: Skills Assessment

Skill assessment is a vital step in the Australian PR process.

Step 3: English Test

IELTS / PTE coaching and mock tests to improve scores.

Step 4: Submit EOI

Submit Expression of Interest for PR invitation.

Step 5: State Nomination

Apply for state nomination for Subclass 190 or 491.

Step 6: Receive ITA

Invitation to Apply received based on your score.

Step 7: Apply for PR

Lodge Australia PR visa application with documents.

Step 8: Medical & PCC

Complete health checkups and police clearance.

Step 9: Wait for Processing

DHA reviews your application and supporting documents.

Step 10: Visa Grant

Receive your Australia PR visa and start your journey.
